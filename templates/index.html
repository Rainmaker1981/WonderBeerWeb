Start here

Follow the steps below to get the best results:

  1. Select your profile — upload or choose a tasting profile with your preferred styles and flavors.
  2. Find local breweries — use the State → City → Venue dropdowns to pick where you’re drinking.
  3. Match beers to your style — once a venue is selected, we’ll match their menu to your profile.

1) Choose Profile

Upload or pick a saved profile. You can download the sample and edit it.

2) Find Breweries

Pick State → City → Venue from pre-filled data. No typing needed.

3) Match Beers

Coming from your selected venue. (Hook this to /match in your repo.)

Tips

  • Place your breweries.csv in the data/ folder (or set the BREWERIES_CSV env var).
  • CSV headers must be: Name, City, State_province.
  • Analytics works out of the box with sample_profile.json.
