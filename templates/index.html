Getting Started

  1. Upload your Untappd CSV and generate a tasting profile → Go to Profile
  2. Find local breweries by Country → State/Province → City → Venue → Find Breweries
  3. Match beers on that venue’s menu using your profile.

Optionally, use Beer Lookup to fetch details from a local cache.
